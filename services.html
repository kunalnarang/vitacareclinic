Our Services

What we offer

We offer medical services covered by Alberta Health, private medical care, refugee health and WCB injuries.

Private Medical Care

Offering primary care services at private cost to patients who do not have medical coverage.

For patients without provincial health coverage, we provide the same standard of family medicine at clear, transparent rates. Visits are unhurried, billing is simple, and there's no waitlist to join.

  • Initial consultations and follow-ups
  • Prescriptions, referrals, lab requisitions
  • Pre-employment and travel medical exams
  • Direct billing for many extended health plans

Refugee Health

Offering comprehensive primary care for health of our refugee population.

Trauma-informed primary care for refugees and newcomers. We coordinate with settlement organizations and provide interpretation services where possible.

  • IFHP-eligible coverage handled directly
  • Vaccinations, screenings, catch-up immunizations
  • Mental health support and specialist connections
  • Interpreter services available on request

WCB Injuries

Helping workers with injuries at work.

Workplace injuries deserve fast, focused care. We assess, treat, and document — and coordinate with the Workers' Compensation Board to keep your claim moving.

  • Initial WCB assessments and paperwork
  • Follow-up visits and progress reports
  • Return-to-work coordination
  • Direct WCB billing — no out-of-pocket costs
