About us

We are a group of family physicians who got our training in family medicine residency from University of Alberta. We are passionate to provide community healthcare to our patients with utmost safety, evidence-base and effectiveness.

We have an on-site pharmacy and a well trained prescribing pharmacist on site to facilitate provision of safe, up-to-date and practical prescribing for our patients' benefits. We are also affiliated with Southside PCN which offers a diverse array of multidisciplinary care to improve patient care and health outcomes.

About VitaCare Clinic

We offer comprehensive compassionate primary care to patients.

Our team of qualified and certified Canadian family Physicians, Family Medicine MDs, are competent and capable of providing care to all adults, children, and elderly.

We are also affiliated with Southside Primary Care Network that offers care from multidisciplinary professions.

We also offer medical services including private medical care, refugee health and WCB injuries.
